Spiral Edit - video editor

This is the beginning code sketch for a video editor
where the timeline is arranged as a rectangular 
spiral. The playhead moves around
the spiral from the center which is the
first clip or frame.

A rectangular spiral UI has a lot of 
potential, first because it removes
fatigue of having to scrub to the desired
position on the timeline because the ui is
instead about zooming out and then
zooming in.  this also demonstrates
the importance of changing the form
of a technology when the surrounding
conditions have changes.  that is,
a timeline is natural for physical
film editing but when the computer display
is available film editing should be
adapted to its conventions, namely
the ability (especially in the last
decade) to zoom in and out.
